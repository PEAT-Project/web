Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the PEAT parking management system.

How does the PEAT parking system work?

PEAT allows you to find, reserve, and pay for parking spots in real-time through our user-friendly app. Simply search for available spots near your destination, book with a tap, and use the app to access the parking facility.

Is PEAT available in my city?

PEAT is currently available in major urban centers locally. We're expanding rapidly - check our coverage map in the app for the most up-to-date information on available locations.

How do I cancel a parking reservation?

You can cancel a reservation through the app up to 30 minutes before your scheduled arrival time for a full refund. Later cancellations may be subject to our cancellation policy.

What payment methods does PEAT accept?

PEAT accepts all major credit cards, debit cards, and mobile payment solutions. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted platform.
